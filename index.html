Our Maintenance Philosophy

Why we don't set a dollar limit on your repairs

It can sound counterintuitive at first. But a fixed approval threshold quietly works against you — and the way we handle repairs instead is built to protect both your investment and your peace of mind. Here's the thinking, and the guardrails behind it.

The Problem With a Hard Number

The rigid-number trap

Picture the chain reaction an arbitrary dollar limit sets off:

1
The vendor is standing in your property — and your tenant took time off work to be there for the appointment.
2
The repair comes in just over an arbitrary limit, so the work has to pause for approval.
3
We can't reach you — or the vendor can't reach us — within the next five minutes. Time is money to a vendor, and they won't wait.
4
The vendor leaves, and still bills a trip charge for showing up.
5
The tenant is frustrated after making the appointment, and the same repair now costs more than the small difference that triggered the call.
A rigid number doesn't protect you — it quietly costs you more.
What We Do Instead

Judgment, with clear guardrails

We don't just open work orders. Every request is analyzed before anyone is dispatched — so the question is never simply "how much," but whether the work should happen at all:

Is it urgent?
Is it a liability risk?
Is it actually the tenant's responsibility?
Nothing falls through the cracks. Minor items that don't justify a vendor trip are logged and batched into a future, more substantial work order — so we're deliberate with your money, and nothing is wasted or forgotten.
Your Protection

The guardrails that keep this in check

A

Large-scale repairs always come to you

Roof, full flooring, appliance or HVAC replacement, major exterior work — these always come to you first, with a quote (or quotes) and options. We proceed only on your authorization.

B

The financial-sense backstop

If a repair simply doesn't make financial sense — even with the vendor already at the property — say a unit needs replacing rather than repairing, that stops the work on its own. We bring the replacement decision to you, to make together.

C

Your 30-day right to terminate, anytime

The latitude lets us do our job well in the moment. This ensures it's never abused — if you ever feel we aren't working in your best interest, you're free to part ways on 30 days' notice.

The Bottom Line
Our interests are aligned. Your property performing well is how we succeed.
